02 · Multimodal product · Head of Product, IPLAB Seoul

    
Gru: a mirror that sees you in 3D.

    

      A 0→1 AI fitness mirror: hardware, software, content, and app. Multi-camera and
      sensor arrays capture body data, generate a live 3D avatar, and coach your pose in
      real time. Filed as a WIPO/USPTO patent (PCT/US2020/061822) and backed
      by $50K+ in non-dilutive grants from KISED and the City of Seoul.
    


    

      
    


    

      

        Capture
        
Multi-camera / sensor array

        
Synchronized cameras and sensors capture full-body pose and movement data.

      

      

        Model
        
Live 3D avatar

        
Body data is reconstructed into a real-time 3D avatar of the user.

      

      

        Coach
        
Pose coaching & styling

        
The avatar drives pose correction and personalized outfit recommendations.

04 · AI video agent · Open source

    
Hesse: AI Video Director Agent in your pocket.

    

      A phone-operated AI video production pipeline built on Claude Code.
      One command from my phone starts the loop: a producer skill turns the idea into
      characters, locations, and a storyboard; Higgsfield generates the
      shots; renders land in Google Drive to approve or revise, while the Mac runs
      unattended. Fully open source.
