01The problem

Paper business cards are one-directional and forgettable. You hand one over, hope it survives the bottom of a bag, and never know if anyone looked. The real work, the follow-up, gets lost in the days after the conversation, when context has already faded.

02What Tabo does

Tabo is a smart NFC card that shares your full digital profile with a single tap, and the receiver needs no app. Better still, the exchange is mutual: both people walk away with each other's details automatically. The whole thing takes about three seconds.

  • Bidirectional exchange, both sides get the contact, not just one
  • AI-drafted follow-ups, a contextual message ready before you've left the room
  • Built-in analytics, profile views, link clicks, and where taps happened
  • Social and portfolio links, Instagram, LinkedIn, and your own site in one place

03Why it matters

A traditional card ends the moment it's handed over. Tabo treats that moment as the start of a relationship: instant two-way contact, an AI nudge to actually follow up, and data showing which connections are warm. Networking that used to take a week of "nice to meet you" emails, done in a tap.
